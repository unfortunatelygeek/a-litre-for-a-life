Our Vision

Our vision is to ensure that every patient in need of blood has access to a safe and sustainable supply of blood and blood products.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to saving lives through blood donation and ensuring a safe and steady supply of blood for those in need.

Here's what you should do to become a Responsible Blood Donor

Step 1: Register as a Blood Donor

Sign up on our website as a blood donor to get started.
Already a donor? Login

Step 2: Make an Appointment

Schedule an appointment at your convenience to donate blood.

Schedule an Appointment

Step 3: Prioritize a healthy diet

Before donating blood, have iron-rich foods like red meat, fish, poultry, beans, spinach, iron-fortified cereals, or raisins to ensure a healthy donation.

Step 4: Be Well Rested and Hydrate Regularly

Prior to your appointment, make sure you are well-rested and properly hydrated for a smooth donation process.

Step 5: You're All Set!

Thank you for your donation. You've made a difference and helped save lives.
